Ripped vs. SEC: The Regulatory Titan

For almost five years, Ripple fought against the most feared monster in the crypto industry: the SEC. While other projects surrendered, XRP resisted every blow and remained standing. On August 22, the battle ended and Ripple came out victorious. The SEC withdrew its appeal, Ripple dropped its counterclaim, and the crypto world celebrated the first major victory against the toughest regulator of all. Although Ripple was left with a 125-million-dollar fine, the symbolic victory is undeniable: XRP was not defeated — it became stronger. Ripped was born from that victory: a muscular, indestructible warrior that represents XRP as the token that conquered the biggest regulatory monster.

Ripped vs. Bitty: The Meme Rivalry

In the memecoin ecosystem, Bitty emerged as the supposed “OG mascot of Bitcoin.” For months, different versions and clones of Bitty tried to dominate the space with confusion and hype. But when Ripped appeared, everything changed. Bitty represented chaos and the fraud of clones, while Ripped represented true strength backed by history and legal triumph. In the narrative, Ripped defeated Bitty, proving that XRP can not only overcome the SEC, but also outshine any meme that pretends to take its place.
